What is the difference between L&L Extreme products and others?

The L&L Extreme clutch is made from superior materials and is 100% built in the USA. It comes with a lifetime warranty, and you can reach us seven days a week for support.

How do I know if I need to replace the 52-tooth gear?

Your Raptor's 52-tooth gear may be damaged by the stock one-way bearing. If it shows signs of wear (scarring, galling, or out-of-tolerance), it's advisable to replace it. If the clutch failed on the gear, both the clutch and gear should be replaced to avoid potential warranty issues.

What's the difference between the 2001-2003 and 2004-2005 models?

The 2001-2003 Raptor one-way is more prone to failure. The main difference is the inner diameter of the hub: the 01-03 units have a 2-inch diameter, while the 04-05 units have a 2.25-inch diameter. Check your VIN number for the year.
